Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Cyber Scripts ALX Solutions SRL (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information in the mobile game Planet Pop - Merge Puzzle Game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, phone number, address, or any direct identifiers.

The game can be played without creating an account and without providing any personal data.

2. Information Collected Automatically

Some non-personal information may be automatically collected by third-party services integrated into the game. This data is used only for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising.

These services may collect:

  • Device type and OS version
  • General location (country/region)
  • In-app events (e.g., session duration, level progress)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)

3. Third-Party Services

The game may use the following third-party providers:

  • Google Play Services – analytics, achievements, game services
  • Unity Ads – showing ads and measuring ad performance

These third parties operate under their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy

Our game is intended for a general audience and does not target children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect user data directly, we rely on trusted platforms (Google, Unity) which implement industry-standard security measures to protect their data.

6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete.

For data handled by third-party services, you may manage advertising preferences on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
