Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Cyber Scripts ALX Solutions SRL (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information in the mobile game Planet Pop - Merge Puzzle Game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, phone number, address, or account credentials.

The game can be played without creating an account and without providing any personal data.

2. Information Collected Automatically

Some non-personal data may be automatically collected by third-party SDKs integrated into the game. This information is used for analytics, crash reporting, and personalized or non-personalized advertising.

These services may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • General location (country/region)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)
  • App interactions and events (e.g., session length, level progress)
  • Crash logs and performance data

No data collected through these services allows us to identify you personally.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

The game may use the following third-party providers:

  • LevelPlay / IronSource – ad mediation and ad performance measurement
  • Unity Ads – displaying rewarded and interstitial ads
  • Google AdMob (if enabled) – advertising and performance analytics
  • Google Play Services – Android app services and diagnostics

These companies may collect data as described in their own Privacy Policies:

  • ironSource / LevelPlay Privacy Policy
  • Unity Privacy Policy
  • Google & AdMob Privacy Policy

Advertising identifiers may be used to deliver relevant ads or to limit the number of times you see the same advertisement. You can reset or limit ad personalization from your device settings.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Third-party services retain data only as long as necessary for their operational purposes, such as analytics or fraud prevention.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect or store data ourselves, we rely on industry-leading partners (Google, Unity, ironSource) that implement strong security measures to protect the information they process.

6. Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take action.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data directly, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete.

For third-party data (ads, analytics), you can manage preferences on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Disable “Personalized ads” if desired

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
